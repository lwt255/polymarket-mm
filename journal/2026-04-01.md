## Critical Findings

### 1. CL Filter Had Lookahead Bias
The 83% WR "Option D" CL filter from March 31 was based on **full-candle** `chainlinkMoveDollars` (open-to-close), but the live bot only knows CL direction at T-30s. ~23% of candles reverse in the final 30 seconds.

- Backtest CL signal: `chainlinkClose - chainlinkOpen` → **82.8% WR** (cheating — uses future data)
- Live bot CL signal: `currentPrice(T-30s) - openPrice` → **67.3% WR** (honest)
- CL contradicts at T-30s: **65.9% WR** (almost identical — CL filter at T-30s has zero edge)

The CL filter was reducing trade volume by ~40% for no WR improvement.

### 2. prevResolution Was Never Fixed in Backfill
The March 29 backfill (`backfill-onchain-resolution.ts`) corrected `resolution` from on-chain but **never touched `prevResolution`**. It still contained the old wrong Gamma API values. 39.2% of prevResolution values were wrong (5,696 out of 14,517).

This means the "prev=dog buy underdog" signal that showed +$2,000/day on backfilled data was an artifact of comparing **wrong** prevResolution against **corrected** resolution.

**Fix**: Built `src/scripts/backfill-prev-resolution.ts` — chains corrected resolution values within each crypto×interval series. Verified 0 mismatches across 14,517 consecutive pairs post-fix.

## Session 6: Two-Layer Strategy — Price Trading + Resolution

### The Fundamental Discovery

We don't need to hold to resolution. Polymarket is a continuous order book — you can buy and sell at any time during the candle. This unlocks a completely different strategy: **bracket orders** (stop loss + profit target), trading price movement rather than binary outcomes.

### Layer 1: Early Price Trading (the big discovery)

**Enter at T-180 (2 minutes into a 5-min candle), place bracket order: stop at -3¢, target at +20¢.**

Key findings:
- **prev=fav filter barely matters** — the bracket works on everything in the 50-80¢ zone
- The -1¢ stop was fake: 95% of trades instantly stopped because median spread is 2¢. **Stop must exceed spread to survive entry.**
- -3¢ stop survives 93% of entries. -5¢ survives 99%.
- **No re-entry needed** — one entry per candle per crypto is enough

### Spread Constraint (Critical Lesson)

The spread at T-180 is typically 1-2¢:
- Spread <= 1¢: only 5% of candles
- Spread <= 2¢: 80%
- Spread <= 3¢: 93%

A stop must be wider than the spread or you get instantly stopped. The -1¢ stop results ($1,600/day) were entirely fake — the bid at entry is already below the stop level. After adding this constraint, results dropped from $1,600 to $433/day. Still strong but honest.

## What Happened

### 1. Proved the Collector Was Wrong
Built `verify-collector-resolution.ts` — samples collector records and checks each against on-chain `payoutNumerators` (the CTF contract that actually pays out money).

Results on 200 records:
- **20.5% of collector resolutions were WRONG**
- Gamma API and Chainlink agree with each other but disagree with on-chain truth
- Errors systematically favored the underdog — creating a phantom edge

The "46% underdog win rate" was actually **13%**. The edge never existed.

### 2. Fixed the Collector
Replaced `resolveOutcome()` in `pricing-collector.ts` to use on-chain `payoutNumerators` instead of Gamma API `outcomePrices`. Now polls the CTF contract directly after each candle. Verified 100% match rate.

Collector restarted with on-chain resolution (PID 39438). First resolution batch showed CL disagreements being caught in real-time:
```
NOTE: OnChain=DOWN CL=UP — using on-chain (actual payouts)
```

### 3. Backfilled All Historical Data
Built `backfill-onchain-resolution.ts` — resolved all 11,449 unique slugs on-chain and rewrote both `pricing-data.jsonl` (12,145 records) and `pricing-data.raw.jsonl`. 16.8% of resolutions were corrected. Originals backed up as `.bak` files.

### 4. Found the Real Edge
With corrected data, ran comprehensive analysis (`corrected-edge-hunt.ts`). The underdog strategy is dead. But **buying the FAVORITE** at 50-65¢ is profitable:

**Portfolio B:**
- ETH 5m favorite 55-65¢ — 71% WR, +11pp edge

10 days of data: **10/10 winning days, 70% WR, $69/day at $10/trade, zero drawdown.**

Deeper microstructure analysis found additional signal: momentum (rising favorite price), previous resolution matching, and US evening hours all boost the edge further.

Key difference from old bot: resolution uses on-chain `payoutNumerators`, not Gamma API.

Key finding: **all 4 post-depth-check unfills were SOL**. ETH and XRP fill fine. SOL's books are thin.

Even worse: **7 of 8 unfilled trades would have been wins**. Left $34 on the table.

Added 1¢ price bump for SOL orders only. Cost: ~$0.14/trade (14 shares × 1¢). If it converts even one unfilled win, that's +$4-5 for 14¢ cost. ETH/XRP/BTC unchanged since they fill fine.

- Worst streak: 5 losses in a row (happened once in 10 days)

Key insight: **drawdowns happen within hours, not across days**. Every day recovered. The 75% WR means the wins always come back — but only if you're still alive when they do.

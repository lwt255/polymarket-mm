## Evening: went live, caught a critical bug, fixed it

### Context
Dry-run at 20 trades showed 75% WR, +$2.08/tr, +14.3pp over buy price — well above collector's +5.99pp ceiling. Parity audit (after streaming rewrite) showed decision snapshots match collector almost perfectly (0.06¢ avg diff), and 16-trade fill drift was ~$0.47/tr. Decided to flip to small-stakes live at `--size 5 --max-loss 25` to measure actual live-vs-collector gap.

### Live run — 2 trades before halt

**Trade 1 (ETH 5m UP @69¢, 5m_spread_tight)**: fired at T-37, filled in 1s, held 75s, won cleanly. Net PnL +$2.01 after ~$0.16 in taker fees. End-to-end cycle verified.

**Trade 2 (SOL 15m UP @61¢, late_flip + price_55_65)**: fired at T-36. Taker order placed, then the bot said:
```
20:59:29  PHANTOM FILL: taker order disappeared but shares not received — treating as UNFILLED
```
Bid crashed to 2¢ in 20 seconds (massive collapse on the UP side). SOL resolved DOWN; bot logged "LOSS | PnL $0.00" because it thought no fill.

Except the wallet balance dropped $4.88 — **exactly the order cost**. The ledger said UNFILLED. The money said FILLED. Silent orphan loss.

### Root cause
Two safety layers failed together:

1. **Phantom-fill verifier too brittle.** Single on-chain share-balance read after a 2s wait. When Polygon block propagation / DRPC flakiness returned 0 shares in that window (DRPC threw HTTP 400s on other calls just seconds later), the verifier declared phantom even though the fill had actually settled.
2. **Reconciliation gated on `status==='FILLED'`.** So a false-positive phantom skipped balance reconciliation entirely. The one safety net that could have caught this — comparing expected PnL vs actual balance delta — was wired not to run on declared-UNFILLED rows. Ledger ended up with `reconciliation: null` and `expectedPnl: 0` while $4.88 drained.

### Fix (commit 788ecdb)

- `verifyFillOnChain` retries 4× over ~12s, distinguishes terminal RPC failure (trust open-orders) from confident "no shares arrived after 12s" (real phantom).
- **Reconciliation now runs on every single-trade candle regardless of declared status.** For UNFILLED rows expected PnL is 0, so any material balance delta trips the $2.50 discrepancy alarm and HALTS. Balance delta is the only ground truth.
- Halt message distinguishes FILLED-with-bad-balance (executor lied about fill) vs UNFILLED-with-balance-delta (false-positive phantom, shares on chain possibly needing manual redemption).

### Why this matters
Without the fix, false-positive phantoms would continue to silently book real losses as $0 in the ledger while the wallet quietly drained. The ledger→audit→decision loop becomes unreliable. With the fix, the worst-case bad-phantom outcome is a loud halt + operator review, not a hidden drain.

### Critical Finding: Loss Pattern Analysis

**Analyzed all 11 wrong predictions across 113 iterations. Three clear patterns:**

#### Pattern 1: Non-Adaptive Checkpoint = Most Losses
- **9 out of 11 wrong predictions used `adaptive=False`**
- Only 2 wrong predictions occurred with adaptive checkpoint enabled
- The adaptive checkpoint (two-point direction check) acts as a critical safety net
- **Rule: Always use adaptive checkpoint**

#### Pattern 2: Overnight Hours Cluster Losses
- 5 of 11 losses occurred in the 1-4 AM window
- BTC price action becomes choppy/non-directional overnight
- Momentum signal reads noise as signal during these hours
- **Rule: Don't trade 1-5 AM**

#### Pattern 3: Higher Loser Bids = Higher Risk
- Most wrong predictions had loser bids of 17-25 cents
- Higher loser bid = more uncertain market = more likely to flip
- The "fattest" trades are counterintuitively the riskiest
- **Consider: Cap maxLoserBidCents at 20c instead of 32c**

#### Pattern 4: Early=60s Dominates Losses
- 8 of 11 losses used 60s early window
- The 90s early window had fewer losses proportionally
- Longer early window gives more time for direction to establish

**Impact**: Rules 1 and 2 alone would have eliminated 7 of 11 wrong predictions without meaningfully reducing winning trades (overnight hours barely produce winners anyway).

### New Feature: Multi-Checkpoint System (Built ~10 PM)

**Key Insight**: User asked "why are we only checking direction twice?" — leading to the realization that the auto research loop can only optimize parameters that exist. It can't invent new features. The two-check adaptive system was hardcoded — the loop could toggle it on/off and adjust timing, but couldn't invent "check 3 times."

**What was built**: Replaced the hardcoded 2-check adaptive system with an N-check system.

**3 new tunable parameters added:**

| Parameter | Range | Purpose |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| `adaptiveCheckpointCount` | 2-5 | Number of evenly-spaced direction checks between early and confirm. ALL must agree or trade is skipped |
| `minAdaptiveMoveDollars` | 0-50 | Minimum BTC move required at EACH checkpoint. Filters out weak/choppy signals at every check point |
| `minFillProbability` | 0-0.9 | Skip trade if estimated fill probability too low, saving gas on likely no-fills |

**How multi-checkpoint works:**
```
adaptiveCheckpointCount=2 (original):
  T-120s: Check 1 "UP"  →  T-25s: Check 2 "UP"  →  Trade!

adaptiveCheckpointCount=4 (new):
  T-120s: Check 1 "UP"  →  T-88s: Check 2 "UP"  →  T-57s: Check 3 "DOWN"  →  Sit out!
```

**Also updated:**
- Starting params locked to proven winners: adaptive=true, early=120s, minMove=$0
- Playbook updated with all findings so AI researcher knows what works
- History table now shows new params so AI can track their impact across experiments

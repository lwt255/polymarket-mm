### Session 4: The Edge is Dying — 365-Day Regime Change Analysis

**Activity**: Extended backtest to 365 days to determine if the Feb-Mar accuracy drop is new or cyclical.

**Verdict: This is new. The edge held for 11 straight months and then collapsed.**

#### 365-Day Monthly Accuracy Trend

| Month | Accuracy | Reversal Rate | BTC Daily Range | Assessment |
|-------|----------|---------------|-----------------|------------|
| Mar 2025 | 94.9% | 9.5% | 3.56% | Strong |
| Apr 2025 | 94.9% | 10.1% | 3.91% | Strong |
| May 2025 | 96.1% | 9.1% | 2.84% | Strong |
| Jun 2025 | 96.5% | 9.0% | 2.59% | Strong |
| Jul 2025 | 97.3% | 8.0% | 2.46% | Peak |
| Aug 2025 | 97.5% | 8.0% | 2.56% | Peak |
| Sep 2025 | 97.7% | 8.2% | 2.01% | Peak |
| Oct 2025 | 95.9% | 9.4% | 3.64% | Strong |
| Nov 2025 | 94.0% | 10.1% | 4.17% | Good |
| Dec 2025 | 95.5% | 9.6% | 3.46% | Strong |
| Jan 2026 | 95.3% | 8.9% | 3.08% | Strong |
| **Feb 2026** | **90.5%** | **13.5%** | **5.51%** | **⚠️ Degrading** |
| **Mar 2026** | **79.1%** | **20.0%** | **4.85%** | **🚨 Collapsed** |

Key observations:
- Strategy was 94-98% accurate for 11 consecutive months (Mar 2025 – Jan 2026)
- No prior weak period exists in the data — this is NOT cyclical
- Reversal rate doubled: 8-10% baseline → 20% in March
- Even the 8+bps "strong signal" band collapsed: 99% (Sep) → 84.6% (Mar)
- The 5-8bps band went from 96.7% (Sep) → 70.5% (Mar)

#### The Reversal Rate Is the Smoking Gun

| Period | Reversal Rate | Interpretation |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| Mar 2025 – Jan 2026 | 8-10% | Stable baseline — BTC momentum holds |
| Feb 2026 | 13.5% | 50% increase — something changed |
| Mar 2026 | 20.0% | Doubled — last-minute reversals are now routine |

One in five candles now reverses in the final minute. For 11 months it was one in ten.

#### What Changed?

The pattern is consistent with the edge becoming public knowledge:
- The buy-winner-at-T-60s signal was discoverable by anyone watching the markets
- If enough participants buy the winner at T-60s, the price overshoots
- Counter-traders can then push price back in the final 30 seconds
- Higher BTC volatility (5.51% daily range in Feb vs 2% in summer) makes last-second reversals easier to execute
- The timing (Jan → Feb transition) suggests bots were deployed in late January

#### Lesson Learned: Edges Don't Last Forever

This is a textbook example of edge decay in markets:
1. Edge exists (momentum at T-60s predicts outcome)
2. Edge is discovered and exploited (bots buy the winner)
3. Exploitation changes the market dynamics (prices overshoot)
4. Counter-strategies emerge (last-second reversals)
5. Edge compresses to near break-even

The 180-day backtest was misleading because it averaged 11 good months with 1-2 bad ones. **The relevant data is the last 4-6 weeks**, and that data says the edge is gone or severely degraded.

#### What Now?

Options to explore:
1. **Monitor only** — Watch reversal rates weekly. If they revert to 10%, the edge may return.
2. **Reversal strategy** — Buy the *loser* at T-60s instead. But 20% reversal rate = 20% accuracy, which is terrible. The market isn't reversing enough for this to work in the opposite direction.
3. **Later entry** — Move to T-30s or T-20s, where direction may be more locked in. But we already know the dominant MM pulls liquidity at T-15s, so fills become impossible.
4. **Different markets** — The 15-min markets, or non-BTC 5-min markets, may not have been arbitraged yet.
5. **Accept it's dead** — Focus energy on the Selective Sniper strategy for regular Polymarket prediction markets, which has proven profitability on a fundamentally different edge.

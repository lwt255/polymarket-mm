## Key Learnings from the Full Research Arc (March 7-9)

1. **Found a real edge**: BTC momentum at T-60s predicted 5-min outcome with 95%+ accuracy for 11 months
2. **Watched it die**: Reversal rate doubled from 8-10% to 15-20% in Feb-Mar 2026
3. **Understood why**: Edge became public knowledge, counter-trading compressed it to break-even
4. **Discovered MM confidence signal**: Loser bid price predicts accuracy perfectly, but doesn't help because cheap loser = expensive winner = no profit
5. **Speed doesn't matter**: MM prices information, not time. No speed edge to exploit.
6. **Market efficiency is real**: On any market with real-time data, the MM with the best infrastructure wins
7. **Niche markets lack volume**: The automatable information-edge markets don't have enough volume to justify the effort

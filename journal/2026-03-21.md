### Filter Performance Update (218 collector markets)

**CL momentum still dominant:**
- CL agrees with underdog: **90.5% win rate**, $83.57/trade, 167% ROI (n=21)
- CL disagrees: 17.3% win rate, -$22.65/trade (n=52)

**Best combo still perfect:**
- 60-80% + ask 30-45¢ + fav weakening + CL agrees: **7W/0L, 100%, $88.83/trade**

**New filter insights with fresh data:**

1. **Volume filter** — Lower volume = MORE profitable
   - Below median: 50% win rate, $27.93/trade (55.9% ROI)
   - Above median: 36.4% win rate, $11.23/trade (22.5% ROI)
   - Confirms thesis: less efficient markets = more mispricing

2. **Previous market outcome** — Strong signal
   - Prev resolved same as underdog: **70% win rate**, $62.96/trade (126% ROI, n=10)
   - Prev resolved opposite: 21.4% win rate, -$15.85/trade (n=14)
   - Streak/momentum effect — if last market went DOWN, current DOWN underdog more likely to win

3. **Entry timing** — T-45s is consistently best ($21.35/trade, 45% win rate), T-180s second ($12.53/trade)

## Session 3: Deep Math Analysis + 9-Signal System + Stops Removed

### Gambling Math Applied to Collector Data

Applied professional gambling mathematics (calibration curves, favorite-longshot bias, Kelly criterion, closing line value concepts) to the full collector dataset.

**Calibration curve** — the market is mispriced at specific price points:
- 55¢: actual 56.8% vs implied 55% → +1.8% edge
- 70¢: actual 73.0% vs implied 70% → +3.0% edge (strongest)
- 65¢: actual 62.4% vs implied 65% → -2.6% edge (TRAP — worse than breakeven)

**Momentum magnitude** — not just rising/falling, but HOW MUCH:
- Small positive momentum (0-2¢): 70.1% WR, $1.04/trade (best)
- Big momentum (>10¢): 67.5%, $0.49/trade (still good)
- Any negative: 58.6%, -$0.54/trade (bad)

**Kelly criterion** confirms 55-60¢ and 70-75¢ are the positive-edge zones. 60-65¢ is negative Kelly — shouldn't bet there.

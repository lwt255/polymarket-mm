### New Signal: Book Depth Asymmetry

The enriched collector's book shape data (depth within 1c/2c/5c per side) unlocked a filter that was impossible before.

**Bid > ask depth on underdog within 5c = 75% win rate, $67.92/trade (n=40)**

This is the live footprint of the wallet research finding. When more bids than asks are stacked on the underdog side, someone is accumulating — and those markets win 3 out of 4 times. Before the enrichment, the collector only had top-of-book depth at a single price level, so this was invisible.

The inverse is also strong: **ask >> bid (2x+) on underdog = 25% win rate, negative PnL**. Clear avoid signal.

### Regime Labels Now Available as Live Filters

The collapse filter is the sharpest negative signal in the data — nearly zero win rate. Should be a hard gate in any live bot.

## The Unfill Problem Is Real

34% of orders going unfilled. Analyzed all 16 unfilled trades: **15 out of 16 would have been wins.** That's $77 left on the table. Balance would be ~$162 instead of $85 if everything filled.

Why it happens: orders placed at best ask, sit on the book, nobody takes the other side in 6 seconds, bot cancels. The book is thin when the market is moving toward the favorite — which is exactly when our trade is most likely to win.

### SOL 1¢ Bump — Proven
Added last night for SOL only. Results:
- Before bump: 33% fill rate (2/6)
- After bump: 88% fill rate (7/8)
- Cost: ~$0.14/trade (14 shares × 1¢)

Hard proof that the bump works.

### Unfills by Crypto
- ETH: 30% unfill, $47 missed (all 7 would have been wins)
- BTC: 57% unfill, $19 missed (all 4 wins)
- SOL: 36% unfill, $11 missed (4W/1L)
- XRP: 0% unfill — no issues

### Two Fixes Applied
1. **1¢ price bump on ALL cryptos** (was SOL-only). Cost ~$0.15/trade, proven to dramatically improve fill rate.
2. **Cancel timeout extended from 6s to 20s**. Orders enter at T-30, candle ends at T-0, so 20s still leaves 10s buffer. Lets orders sit longer for someone to take the other side.

## Key Insight

The edge exists. The fills are the bottleneck. Every improvement to fill rate directly translates to profit. The 1¢ bump is the cheapest money we've ever spent — $0.14 to rescue $5+ wins.

### The Big Find: Chainlink Confirmation
Tested every untested signal in the data: CL direction, depth ratio, spread, drift magnitude, day of week, consecutive resolutions.

**Chainlink confirmation was a game-changer:**
- CL confirms favorite direction: **83% WR, +19pp buffer, $300/day**
- CL contradicts favorite: **46% WR, -18pp, -$192/day**

This single signal nearly triples the daily PnL. It also rescued losing zones — BTC 5m 55-60¢ goes from -$13/day to +$12/day with CL gate. SOL 5m 60-65¢ goes from -$6/day to +$17/day.

### Option D: The Simplified Bot
CL confirmation is so powerful that per-crypto zone rules aren't needed. The bot simplifies to:

**Any crypto. Any interval. Favorite at 50-80¢. Chainlink confirms. Trade.**

Backtest: 103 trades/day, 83% WR, $298/day at $10/trade, 12/12 winning days. Max drawdown -$68 at $10/trade. Max loss streak: 4.

### Why CL Works
The favorite's price reflects the market's bet. Chainlink shows what the underlying is actually doing. When both agree, the market is right 83% of the time. When they disagree, the market is wrong 54% of the time. We were giving back all our profits on the disagreement trades.

The CL feed was connected to the bot the whole time from the old underdog code. We just never used it as a trade filter.
